Welcome to HooksJS.

This is a simple little project that gives you a Hooks object for registering actions and filters for your scripts. It is loosely based on the WordPress hooks system (hence similar naming) and from other implementations that I have seen around.

Background

This project originally started its life as a gist. It was a proof of concept for creating WordPress like hooks in JavaScript.

Plans

To be honest, the WordPress legacy is nice because it makes the concept clear for WordPress developers, however, I am not overly fond of the method names and I am most likely going to change a number of things. For the sake of backwards compatibility, I will keep the original four methods, but map them to newer methods.
